Why Factory Audit

Go Beyond Basic Verification

For high-value orders, ongoing partnerships, or compliance-sensitive products — a deep-dive audit is essential. We assess what other agents miss.

Production Capacity

Equipment count, line capacity, monthly output, and scalability for your forecast volumes.

Quality Management

ISO 9001 compliance, QC procedures, defect tracking, corrective action processes, and certifications.

Compliance Verification

BSCI, SEDEX, SA8000, ISO 14001, OHSAS 18001 — social and environmental compliance audit.

R&D Capability

In-house design team, mold shop, prototyping, and ability to handle OEM/ODM development.

Supply Chain

Raw material sources, sub-supplier dependencies, and supply chain resilience assessment.

Financial Health

Years in business, customer concentration, export markets, and financial stability indicators.

Audit Process

4-Step Factory Audit Workflow

From booking to final report — 2-3 days on-site plus 2 days reporting.

1

Audit Scope Definition

We define audit criteria: quality, social, environmental, or full combination.

2

On-Site Audit (2-3 days)

Lead auditor + specialists visit factory, interview management, inspect lines.

3

Findings & Scoring

60+ point scoring matrix, risk classification, photo/video evidence collection.

4

Audit Report

30-50 page report with score, findings, corrective actions, and re-audit schedule.

Audit Coverage

Audit

Industry-recognized audit methodology.

Production: Equipment list, age, capacity, maintenance schedule
Quality: ISO certification, IQC/IPQC/OQC process, calibration records
HR: Worker count, turnover rate, training records, working hours
Compliance: Fire safety, building permits, environmental licenses
Trade: Export license, customs record, major customer list
R&D: Design team, sample room, mold workshop, testing equipment
Social: BSCI/SEDEX audit, no child labor, fair wages, safety
