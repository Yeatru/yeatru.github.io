What Our Audit Covers

A 60+ point inspection across 7 key dimensions.

Legal Status: Business license, tax registration, export license
Facility: Real factory vs. trading company, ownership, location
Production Capacity: Equipment, lines, workforce, monthly output
Quality System: ISO 9001, in-line QC, final inspection process
Workforce: Headcount, training, working conditions, safety
Trade History: Major customers, export markets, years in business
Reputation: Court records, customer complaints, market feedback
