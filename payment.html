    PAYMENT TERMS

    Secure & Flexible Payment

    Multiple payment options to suit your business needs

    Bank Transfer (T/T)

    Secure wire transfer directly to our company bank account. Most cost-effective for larger orders.

    PayPal

    Fast and secure PayPal payments. Ideal for sample orders and small batch purchases.

    Western Union

    Quick money transfer service. Convenient for urgent payments and smaller amounts.

    WorldFirst (万里汇)

    Secure cross-border payment via WorldFirst. Competitive exchange rates with fast and reliable fund transfers worldwide.

    Standard Payment Terms

    We work with standard payment terms that protect both parties and ensure smooth transactions.

    30% Deposit

    30% deposit to start production after order confirmation.

    70% Balance Before Shipping

    70% balance payment after final inspection, before goods are shipped.

    Quality Guarantee

    If quality issues are found, we work with suppliers to resolve or provide compensation.

    Transparent Process

    All payments are handled with full transparency and integrity.

    Recommended for New Clients

    WorldFirst (万里汇)

    For new clients, we recommend WorldFirst for maximum protection. It's secure, fast, and offers competitive exchange rates for international transactions.

    • Secure cross-border payments
    • Competitive exchange rates
    • Fast fund transfers
    • Multi-currency support
    • Buyer protection

    Note: We typically work with 30% deposit + 70% balance before shipping. For new clients, we recommend WorldFirst for secure cross-border payments.
