Multiple payment options to suit your business needs

Secure wire transfer directly to our company bank account. Most cost-effective for larger orders.

PayPal

Fast and secure PayPal payments. Ideal for sample orders and small batch purchases.

Western Union

Quick money transfer service. Convenient for urgent payments and smaller amounts.
