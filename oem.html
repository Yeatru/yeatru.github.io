Our Process

4 Steps from Concept to Branded Product

Typically 2-4 weeks from requirements to sample delivery.

1

Submit Requirements

Send your product idea, logo file (AI/PSD), packaging concept, and target quantity. We reply within 4 hours.

2

Design & Mold

Our team creates 2D/3D mockups. For private molds, we finalize tooling drawings. You approve before production.

3

Sample Production

Custom sample produced in 3-7 days. Photo and video report sent for your approval before mass production.

4

Mass Production

Once sample approved, production starts immediately. QC at every stage. Delivery in 15-30 days.
