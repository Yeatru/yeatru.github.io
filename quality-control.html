Inspection Standards

AQL 2.5 / 4.0 Sampling

Industry-standard quality levels we apply.

AQL 2.5: Major defects (functional, safety, visible)
AQL 4.0: Minor defects (cosmetic, finish, size variance)
Critical Defects: Zero tolerance (safety, hazardous, illegal)
Sampling Plan: Based on lot size, ANSI/ASQ Z1.4 standard
Defect Classification: Critical / Major / Minor categories
Photo Documentation: All defects photographed in HD
Re-inspection: Free if first inspection fails and rework is done
