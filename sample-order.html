How It Works

4 Steps to Get Your Samples

From inquiry to sample in your hands — typically 10-20 days total.

1

Submit Requirements

Tell us which products you need samples for. Provide specs, target quality level, and any customization notes. Quote within 4 hours.

2

Supplier Coordination

We contact the factory, confirm sample specs, and arrange production. No extra coordination needed from you.

3

Sample Inspection

We receive samples at our warehouse, photograph and inspect quality. Photo report sent to you for approval.

4

Express Delivery

Once approved, we consolidate with other samples and ship via express. Door-to-door delivery with tracking.

Sample Fees

Transparent Sample Pricing

No hidden fees. Every sample cost clearly explained upfront.

Standard Sample Fee

$30-150 per SKU
  • Production: Factory production cost + our handling fee
  • Included: 1 sample unit, basic packaging, photo report
  • Shipping: Express shipping quoted separately ($30-80 worldwide)

OEM/Custom Sample Fee

$50-300 per SKU
  • Production: Custom mold/tooling (one-time) + sample production
  • Included: Custom sample + design mockup (if first order), photo/video report
  • Shipping: Express shipping quoted separately
  • Sample fee refundable with bulk order of 1,000+ pcs

Consolidation: FREE

Combine samples from multiple suppliers into one shipment. Save up to 60% on shipping costs.
